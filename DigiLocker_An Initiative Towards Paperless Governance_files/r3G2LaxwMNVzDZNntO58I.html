How to use the assistant

A. Formulate Your Query
a. Be Specific: The more precise your question, the better the chatbot can understand your needs and provide relevant results.
i. Example: Instead of "Tell me about contracts," try "What are the essential elements of a valid contract under the Indian Contracts Act, 1872?"
b. Use Legal Terminology: Employing accurate legal terms (e.g., "jurisdiction," "limitations," "breach of contract") will enhance the chatbot's ability to interpret your request.
B. Specify Relevant Information: Include details such as:
  • Jurisdiction: The specific State where the law applies.
  • Area of Law: The relevant legal field (e.g., contract law, family law, criminal law, constitutional law, commercial law).
  • Relevant Documents: If applicable, mention any specific statute or rule or regulation, etc. (e.g., “Specific Relief Act” or “Aadhaar (Authentication and Offline Verification) Regulations, 2021” or “Uttar Pradesh Legal Metrology (Enforcement) Rules 2011”) related to your query.
    • C. Interact with the Chatbot
  • Ask Clear Questions: Pose your question directly to the chatbot.
  • Review and Refine: The chatbot may provide initial results. Review them carefully. If they are not accurate or complete, refine your query or ask follow-up questions.
