Council for the Result of University Of Rajasthan

University of Rajasthan is issuing Mark Sheets, Provisional Certificates, and Migration Certificates digitally through DigiLocker for students enrolled in 3-Year Degree Programs such as B.A., B.Sc., and B.Com. These official academic documents are available for the years 2000 to 2025 and can be securely accessed by logging into the DigiLocker portal using an Aadhaar-linked mobile number. Students can search for University of Rajasthan under the Education section in DigiLocker, enter the required academic details, and instantly retrieve their digitally signed certificates, which are legally valid for all academic, employment, and official verification purposes
